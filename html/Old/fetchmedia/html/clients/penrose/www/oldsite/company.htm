Company Information        

Penrose Financial Services and its affiliated companies combine to form the Penrose Group, serving investors and institutions in both public and private sectors of Commercial Real Estate.

Penrose originates mortgage loans for a variety of commercial property types including: 
Apartments,         Shopping Centers,            Office Buildings,   
Warehouses,       Mixed Use Properties,      Hotels,
Assisted Living,               Multi-Property Portfolios,                  Flex/Office/Warehouse facilities.

Key individuals with Penrose have been financing property in the Washington, DC region since 1984.  Over the years, those individuals and the associates who have joined them have arranged a large volume and significant variety of
                permanent mortgage loans,
                construction and takeout loans, and
                large equity capital placements.  

By constantly finding investment and financing opportunities for institutional investors throughout all of the challenging phases of the past ten years, our successive operational entities have evolved into the Penrose Group of companies.  

Consulting Services.   Penrose also offers an extensive array of consulting services related to the acquisition and operation of commercial properties.  These services include the financial / operational analysis of properties and business entities.  Other Penrose Group entities provide a variety of strategic corporate real estate services to institutional clients.
