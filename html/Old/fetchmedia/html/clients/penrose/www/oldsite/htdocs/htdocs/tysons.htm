Tysons Corner, Virginia

Tysons Corner, Virgina is the largest suburban office submarket in the Washington, DC metropolitan area with over 20 million square feet of office space. The only larger submarket in the entire region is Washington, DC’s central business district. Developed over the past 25 years, its varying product types reflect the relatively long years of growth. The WestGate and WestPark developments that were constructed between 1967 and 1978 are generally older single tenanted buildings. These developments account for approximately 6 million square feet of space. The remaining 13 million plus square feet of space was constructed between 1978 and the present. Its 20 million square feet of space, as a Central Business District , would be ranked in the top 15 in size nationally. It is larger than Houston, Miami, New Orleans, San Diego and St. Louis central business districts. Over 58 associations are located in Tysons Corner, as well as many major corporation and financial institutions including, Freddie Mac, MCI, First Union Bank, SAIC, American Online, KPMG Peat Marwick, Coopers Lybrand and others.

Tysons Corner is a balanced and mature urban environment, and in addition to its large commercial office base, it contains a significant amount of retail and residential development as well. Tysons Corner and its immediate area have over 34 shopping centers encompassing over 45 million square feet of retail space. Included in this is the 1.8 million square foot Tysons Corner Center whose anchors are Nordstorms, Bloomingdales, The Hecht Company, JC Penny and Lord & Taylor. Across Chain Bridge Road (VA Route 123) is the Galleria at Tysons Corner (a.k.a Tysons II) which is 800,000 square feet and whose anchors are Macy’s, Saks Fifth Avenue and Neiman Marcus. Along Route 7 is the trophy property Fairfax Square a mixed use office/retail development contain such retail tenants as Tiffany and Mortons of Chicago. There are also 11 hotels in Tysons Corner containing over 3,400 rooms with most major hotel operators having a presence in Tysons Corner.
