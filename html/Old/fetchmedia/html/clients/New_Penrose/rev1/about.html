Company Overview

The Penrose Group (“Penrose”) is comprised of four companies, each with a specific area of expertise. These companies are, Penrose Partners, Penrose Real Estate Advisory Services, Penrose Financial Services and Capital Hospitality Group. The companies that comprise Penrose have experience in land acquisition, development, entitlement, zoning, asset management, finance, leasing, sales and workouts. Penrose has the staff and experience to manage an asset from predevelopment through stabilization and disposition. In reviewing the resumes of the Penrose professionals you will note that all of the key personnel have extensive experience in their respective disciplines.
