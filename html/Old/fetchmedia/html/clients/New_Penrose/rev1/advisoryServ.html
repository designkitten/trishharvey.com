Penrose Real Estate Advisory Services

Penrose Real Estate Advisory Services is uniquely qualified to assist lenders and owners of distressed real estate by providing assistance and advice regarding challenges and opportunities to maximize real estate value during these troubled economic times. Unlike firms recently formed to provide similar services, Penrose will be operating in a market with which Penrose has in depth experience. In the 1990”s, Penrose was one of the largest advisors to the FDIC, the RTC, banks and life insurance companies. While based in Washington, DC, Penrose successfully completed assignments throughout the United States. In addition to providing advisory services Penrose has the capability to invest in projects, and to oversee and manage all phases from completion of construction to stabilization and disposition. Penrose Real Estate Advisory Services provides consulting services to banks, developers and corporations with regard to their real estate decisions and acts as an inhouse real estate executive on a retainer basis. The company’s significant experience in all types of commercial property development and management enables it to bring an ownership perspective to the successful analysis of complex real estate situations, to recommend solutions and to implement decisions in an organized and timely manner. The affiliated commercial brokerage group specializes in the sales and leasing of commercial, industrial, office, hotel, undeveloped land and investment properties. In addition to third party brokerage, Penrose assists its investment partners in their acquisitions and disposition needs. Penrose’s staff has the experience and expertise to successfully handle the most complicated transactions in all areas of commercial real estate.
