Michael Moriarty
Director of Hospitality Consulting

Mr. Moriarty is a Managing Partner with Capital Hospitality Group of Herndon, Virginia. He is a past Vice President of Marriott Corporation where he spent fifteen years serving in Finance, Operations and Brand Management positions. Mr. Moriarty also held the position of President of Studio Plus Hotels of Lexington, Kentucky and as President of Travel Services Inc. Of Del Ray Beach, Florida. He holds a BA degree from Iona College and an MBA from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. Mr. Moriarty is also a Certified Public Accountant.
