Document Categories

The Freedom of Information Act Office is responsible for management oversight of the Army-wide implementation of the Freedom of Information Act (FOIA) programs in accordance with 5 USC, and Public Law 106-554.

(a)(2)(A) Records

Final opinions and orders made in the adjudication of cases that may be cited, used, or relied upon as precedents in future adjudications.

Final Opinions, Final Orders

 

(a)(2)(B) Records

Statements of policy and interpretations that have been adopted by the agency and are not published in the Federal Register.

Statements of Policy, Interpretations of Policy

 

(a)(2)(C) Records

Administrative staff manuals and instructions, or portions thereof, that establish DoD policy or interpretations of policy that affect the public.

Manuals, Instructions, Guides, Regulations

 

(a)(2)(D) Records

Records released to the public, under the FOIA, that are or will likely become the subject of subsequent requests.

Criminal Investigation Records, Contracts, Army Regulation 15-6 Investigation, Sexual Harassment in the Army, Intelligence Documents, Discrimination and Racial Conflict, FOIA Annual Reports, Corporal Patrick Tillman, Report on Unidentified Flying Objects, Espionage, Project Canasta Deck
